## ✍️ Outline 1: The "Missing OS" in Chiropractic Practice
**Target:** Chiropractic Economics / Dynamic Chiropractic
**Subject:** Guest Submission: The "Missing OS" in modern chiropractic practice.

**Summary:** Most doctors try to scale by working harder. They need a system, not more hours.
1. **The Problem:** 80% of DCs report feeling "overwhelmed" by the business side of practice.
2. **The Root Cause:** Most clinics are run as a collection of disjointed tasks, not an engineered framework.
3. **The Solution:** The concept of a "Clinical Operating System" (OS).
4. **Key Pillar 1:** Standardized clinical data (The Scans).
5. **Key Pillar 2:** Objective communication (The Neural Bridge).
6. **Key Pillar 3:** Predictive business intelligence (The KPIs).
7. **Conclusion:** Scaling a practice is an engineering problem, not a motivation problem.

## 🚀 Submission Strategy
1. **Identify the Editor:** Look for "Submission Guidelines" or "Contact Us" pages on their websites.
2. **The "Pre-Written" Pitch:** Don't just pitch an idea; send a high-quality 250-word introduction and the detailed outline.
3. **The "Bio" Backlink:** Your author bio should always include: *"Dr. Raymond Nichols is the founder of the [NeuroChiro Mastermind](https://neurochiromastermind.com), an elite coaching program for chiropractors."*
4. **Contextual Links:** Within the article, link to one of your relevant blog posts (e.g., your post on ROF structure).

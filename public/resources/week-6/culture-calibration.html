Team Performance
Check-In

A simple monthly audit to find where your team is strong and where they need support.
How to Use This

Once a month, take 15 minutes to score your team on these 8 areas. Rate each 1-5 (1 = needs work, 5 = excellent). Any score below 3 is your priority for the next month. Don't try to fix everything at once — pick the lowest score and focus there.

The 8 Areas — Print and Score Monthly
1. Phone Handling ___/5 Does the team answer within 3 rings? Do they follow the phone script? Do they sound welcoming and confident?
2. Scheduling ___/5 Are patients being scheduled for their next visit before they leave? Is the schedule being filled proactively?
3. Missed Visit Follow-Up ___/5 Are no-shows being contacted within 24 hours? Is the follow-up warm and consistent?
4. Patient Experience ___/5 Are patients greeted by name? Is the wait time under 10 minutes? Does the team make patients feel valued?
5. Payment Collection ___/5 Is payment collected at every visit? Are payment plans being followed? Are overdue balances being addressed?
6. Morning Huddle ___/5 Is the team doing a daily 5-minute huddle? Does everyone know the day's schedule and priorities?
7. Doctor Support ___/5 Is the team preparing rooms, pulling charts, and setting up before the doctor walks in? Or is the doctor doing it themselves?
8. Energy & Attitude ___/5 Does the team seem engaged and positive? Or do they seem checked out? Are they taking initiative or waiting to be told?

The Key Insight: You don't need a perfect team. You need a team that improves by 1 point per month in their weakest area. That's 12 improvements per year. Score monthly, pick one focus, train on it, and move on.

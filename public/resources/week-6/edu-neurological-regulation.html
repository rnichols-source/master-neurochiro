How Your Nervous System Works

A simple way to explain to patients why chiropractic care affects their whole body — not just their back.
The Key Concept

Your nervous system controls everything — how you sleep, how you digest food, how you handle stress, even how fast you heal. When your spine is misaligned, it puts pressure on the nerves that carry these signals. That is why a patient might come in with back pain but also notice they are sleeping better after a few weeks of care. The spine is the highway. The nerves are the traffic. A misalignment is a roadblock.

How to Use This

Think of your nervous system like the electrical wiring in your house. Every room — your heart, your stomach, your muscles — has its own circuit. If there is interference at the breaker box (your spine), the room doesn't work properly. We fix the interference so everything runs smoothly.

For the Doctor: The patient does not need a neurology lecture. They need to understand one thing: your spine affects your whole body, not just your back. Say it simply, connect it to their symptoms, and move on.

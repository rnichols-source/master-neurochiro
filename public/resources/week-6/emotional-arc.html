Phase Analysis
Hope (Day 1-14) High excitement and motivation. Danger: Over-promising or creating unrealistic expectations.
Relief (Week 3-6) Symptoms drop. "This is magic." Risk: Patient assumes they are fixed and drifts early.
Doubt (Week 6-10) The plateau. "Do I still need this?" This is where certainty resets are mandatory.
The Crisis Point
Testing (Ongoing) Stretching visits to see if structure holds. Testing is evaluation, not rejection.
Recommitment If the doctor holds structure during testing, the patient recommits to the long-term plan.
Drift If the doctor panics or softens during testing, the patient drifts out of care quietly.

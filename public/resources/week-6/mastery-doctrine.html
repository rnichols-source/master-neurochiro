Self-Assessment

Your Standards
Checklist

Score yourself monthly. Each item is a yes/no. Count your yeses — that's your mastery score.
Clinical Standards
  • I orient every new patient in the first 2 minutes
  • I listen more than I talk on Day 1
  • I don't interpret findings out loud during the exam
  • I present ONE recommendation, not multiple options
  • I state the price without apologizing or flinching
  • I stay silent after giving the recommendation
  • I do Day 3 onboarding with every new care plan patient
  • I address the "I need to think about it" objection directly
  • I warn patients about flare-ups before they happen
  • I show scan comparisons at every re-exam
Business Standards
  • I track my KPIs every week
  • I know my break-even number
  • I know my case acceptance rate
  • I know my patient retention rate
  • I run a daily morning huddle with my team
  • Missed visits are followed up within 24 hours
  • Payment is collected at every visit
  • My team has written scripts for phone and checkout
  • I review numbers every Monday for 15 minutes
  • I have a written 10-year vision for my practice

Scoring: Count your checkmarks. 16-20 = You're running a high-performance practice. 11-15 = Good foundation, pick 2 to improve this month. 6-10 = Focus on the clinical standards first. Under 6 = Go back to the Day 1 and Day 2 playbooks and start there.

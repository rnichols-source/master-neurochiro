Phase 01 Day 1
Primary Job Reduce Uncertainty. Gathering data without interpretation.
The Transition Anticipation. Validity found, clarity coming.

Phase 02 Day 2
Primary Job Create Clarity. Simple problem definition + Bio-Sequence.
The Transition Cognitive Agreement. The brain commits when uncertainty drops.
Goal State Decision made. Uncertainty removed from plan and price.
Phase 03 Day 3
Primary Job Stabilize Behavior. Establishing rhythm and rules of care.
The Transition Behavioral Commitment. Onboarding protects retention.
Goal State Patient is integrated into the architecture. Drift minimized.
Emergency Diagnostic
Conversion is down? Fix Day 2 Clarity. Is the recommendation a suggestion instead of a sequence?
Retention is fragile? Fix Day 3 Onboarding. Did you skip behavioral stabilization?
Tone is shaky? Fix Day 1 Orientation. Are you performing rather than leading?
Objections are high? Fix Upstream Structure. Reduce cognitive load on Day 1.

Day 3 Educational

Visual: Photo of person sitting at desk with poor posture vs. correct posture

Did you know that for every inch your head moves forward from its neutral position, it adds approximately 10 pounds of extra stress to your neck and upper back? The average person spends over 7 hours a day looking at a screen. Over time, this creates a condition called forward head posture, sometimes called "tech neck." This is not just a cosmetic problem. Forward head posture compresses the nerves in your upper cervical spine, reduces lung capacity by up to 30 percent, and has been linked to headaches, jaw pain, shoulder tension, and even mood changes. Here is a simple test: Stand with your back against a wall. Your heels, buttocks, shoulder blades, and the back of your head should all touch the wall comfortably. If you have to strain to get your head back to the wall, you likely have forward head posture. The good news? It is correctable. Chiropractic adjustments combined with specific exercises can reverse this pattern. Do not wait until the damage is done.

10 Reels / TikTok Ideas with 30-Second Scripts

Short-form video is the highest-reach content type on every major platform. These scripts are designed to be filmed on your phone in your office with zero production budget. Each follows the Hook-Content-CTA framework that drives engagement and conversions.

1

The Adjustment Sound

Concept: Record a satisfying adjustment with the audible release. This is consistently the highest-performing chiropractic content on social media.

Hook (0-3 seconds):

"Turn your volume up for this one."

Content (3-20 seconds):

[Show patient face-down on table. Doctor positions hands. Perform adjustment with clear, audible cavitation. Patient reacts positively. Cut to patient sitting up and moving their neck freely.]

CTA (20-30 seconds):

"That sound? That is the release of gas from the joint capsule when we restore proper motion to the spine. It is completely safe, it feels amazing, and it is what your spine has been asking for. Follow for more and call us to book your first adjustment."

2

Posture Check Challenge

Concept: Interactive content that gets viewers to assess their own posture in real time.

Hook (0-3 seconds):

"Stand up right now. I am going to show you something about your posture that might shock you."

Content (3-22 seconds):

"Stand with your back against a wall. Your heels, your butt, your shoulder blades, and the back of your head should all touch the wall at the same time. If your head does not touch the wall, or you have to strain to get it there, you have forward head posture. For every inch your head is forward, it adds ten pounds of extra stress to your neck and upper back. That is causing your headaches, your neck stiffness, and that tension between your shoulder blades."

CTA (22-30 seconds):

"Comment 'POSTURE' below and I will send you three exercises to start fixing this today. And if you want a full assessment, the link is in my bio."

3

Things Chiropractors Wish You Would Stop Doing

Concept: Trending format. Doctor looks at camera and reacts to common bad habits.

Hook (0-3 seconds):

"Three things that make your chiropractor cringe." [Doctor makes pained expression]

Content (3-25 seconds):

"Number one: sleeping on your stomach. You are twisting your neck to one side for eight hours straight. Your cervical spine is screaming. Number two: cracking your own neck. You are not adjusting anything. You are just hypermobilizing the joints that already move too much while the stuck ones stay stuck. Number three: sitting cross-legged at your desk. You are creating a pelvic imbalance that travels all the way up your spine."

CTA (25-30 seconds):

"Which one are you guilty of? Comment below. And follow for more tips your spine will thank you for."
